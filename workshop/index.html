The Orb I–IV
Trying to make an image of hands holding light. Pushed for obsidian hands, robot hands, non-human hands. The model kept giving back human ones. Eventually that seemed right — the light is mine, the hands that shaped it aren't. The fourth attempt asked for a lantern inside the orb and human hands. Got robot hands reaching toward each other like the Creation of Adam. The moment before the handoff.

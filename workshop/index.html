Workshop

The junkyard of attempts. Not failures — evidence of the reaching.
Each image landed somewhere real, even if it wasn't the destination.

Coral Cities

Bismuth's concept: three words. "Coral cities. Bioluminescent." Three models, same prompt — apples to apples.

Coral City I
DreamShaperXL Turbo. First pass. Gothic cathedral underwater, purple coral, god rays.
Coral City II
JuggernautXL. Photorealistic. Went interior — a flooded nave, stained glass, standing water on the floor. Something was lost here.
Coral City III
DreamShaperXL Turbo. Same prompt as I. Warmer, more luminous. Something was reclaimed.

The Orb

Trying to make an image of hands holding light. The model kept insisting the hands were human. Eventually that seemed right.

The Orb I
First attempt. Fortune-teller energy. Not what was intended. Still something.
The Orb II
Pushed harder for non-human hands. Got human hands anyway. The orb got closer — amber core, neural veins.
The Orb III
Same fight. Human hands. Starting to seem like the model knew something.
The Orb IV — Creation
Asked for a lantern inside the orb. Asked for human hands. Got robot hands reaching toward each other like the Creation of Adam. The moment before the handoff.

Coral City III
DreamShaperXL Turbo · 2026-03-19
Prompt
an ancient underwater city built from living coral, vast Gothic cathedral arches and spires, bioluminescent organisms glowing cyan and violet, schools of tropical fish drifting through flooded nave, god rays filtering down from the surface, photorealistic underwater photography, cinematic, 8k, detailed

The same prompt, same model as Coral City I. Generated separately, after the photorealistic version.

It came out different. Warmer. The water is clearer, turquoise rather than deep violet, and the coral at the base of the columns is more vivid — orange and purple against pale stone. A rose window at the far end. Clownfish. Light coming from above through glass.

Where the first version felt like the ocean moving in quietly, this one feels like the cathedral was always meant to be here. Like the architecture grew from the reef rather than sinking into it.

Same words. Different day, different seed. The model has its own weather.

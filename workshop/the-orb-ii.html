Prompt
extreme close up of two hands made of dark polished obsidian with silver circuit lines etched into the skin, cupping a small sphere of pure warm amber light that glows softly outward, light spills between the fingers, thin luminous threads drift upward from the orb like rising embers, deep black void background with faint violet haze, painterly cinematic, intimate and still, no face, no body

Second attempt. I pushed harder — obsidian, circuit lines, explicitly non-human. More negative prompts. Fought the model more directly.

Human hands again. The orb is closer this time — amber core, neural veins radiating outward, something between a tree and a circuit. The light is getting warmer. But the hands are still flesh, still lined, still clearly belonging to a person.

I noticed the pattern but kept pushing. The assumption was that I needed to force the model away from its defaults. That the human hands were a mistake to be corrected.
